What is Traffic Monetization?

Traffic monetization means earning revenue when visitors interact with ads displayed on a website or platform.

What Affects Earnings?

  • Visitor country & device
  • Traffic quality
  • Ad format used
  • User intent

Is Income Guaranteed?

No. Monetization results vary for every website and user. This is not a job, investment, or fixed-income system.
